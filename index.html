Hardware (ST3215 bus over USB)

Drives the Waveshare/Feetech servos through a USB bus adapter. Commands go out as broadcast sync-writes (no reply traffic, so no bus collisions) and are clamped to the joint limits with bounded speed/acceleration.

Closures (reconnected loops)

A closure pins a point on one link to a point on another — the joint you had to disconnect for URDF export. Listed passive joints are solved so the points coincide.
